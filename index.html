Temaki

Creating simple lists

Temaki is a simple way of creating quick lists on your Android phone.

Inspired by remembering orders for meals at restaurants, Temaki provides a straightforward way for creating multiple lists of just about anything.

Get started now and manage your lists or tasks on the go.

Focus

Manage things. One at a time.

Focus is a feature in Temaki that lets you keep track of one thing at a time.

Focus on your most important things in life like:

  • Your most important task for the day
  • Important upcoming events
  • Your favourite song, movie, or book
